Gulu Gulu

From Wikipedia ,the free encyclopedia

Gulu Gulu is a 2022 Indian Tamil-language road action comedy film written and directed by Rathna Kumar and produced by Raj Narayanan's banner Circle Box Entertainment.[2] The film was distributed by Udhayanidhi Stalin under his banner Red Giant Movies .[1] The film features Santhanam and Athulya Chandra in the lead roles. The film's music is composed by Santhosh Narayanan with cinematography handled by Vijay Kartik Kannan and editing done by Philomin Raj. The film was released theatrically on 29 July 2022 and received mixed reviews from critics.[3][4]

Premise [edit]

The protagonist does every odd job for survival. He is approached for help by a group of youngsters who want to rescue one of their friends from a kidnap. But that kidnap had happened due to mistaken identity.[5]
